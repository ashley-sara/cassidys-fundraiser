Her Story

Meet Cassidy

Cassidy has always had a heart for helping children feel seen, safe, and supported. Her path has led her toward a career in child development, with a special focus on helping children who have experienced trauma.

Now, she has the opportunity to complete a professional internship in Buffalo, New York. This internship is more than a temporary move. It is a major step toward completing the licensure requirements for the career she has worked so hard to build.

Moving across the country for this opportunity comes with real expenses, including housing, travel, food, transportation, and daily needs. That is why Cassidy’s family and community are coming together to help make this climb possible.

This fundraiser is called Worth the Climb because every step matters. Every donation, every homemade gift purchased, and every encouraging message helps Cassidy move closer to the future she is working toward.

Thank you for walking this trail with her. 🌸

Good to Know

Questions & Answers

What is this fundraiser for?
This fundraiser helps Cassidy cover the costs of moving to Buffalo, New York, and completing her professional internship.
What expenses will donations help cover?
Donations may help with travel, housing, groceries, transportation, daily essentials, and internship-related costs.
Can I donate a custom amount?
Yes. Any amount helps and is deeply appreciated.
Can I support Cassidy without donating?
Yes. Sharing the fundraiser, purchasing a homemade gift, or sending Cassidy encouragement are all meaningful ways to help.
Are donations tax-deductible?
Unless otherwise stated, donations should be considered personal gifts and may not be tax-deductible.
